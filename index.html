Projects

  • Single cell RNA sequencing

    Since January 2023, I have been working under Dr. Dylan McCreedy in the Texas A&M Biology Department. I have developed a workflow to analyze neutrohpils in publicly avalable single-cell RNA-seq data. This work will result in a first-author publication.

  • Janzen-Connell Dynamics:

    While at UT Austin, I developed a mathematical model under Dr. Caroline Farrior. This model simulated how specialist naturalist enemies combat ecological drift. Namely, we examined how the levels of enemy specialization necessary to combat drift in communities of varying sizes.

  • curatoR

    I spent a summer working under Dr. Daniel Spalink in the Texas A&M Herbarium. I began the development of a new R package known as "curatoR". The purpose of this package is to provide easy methods for scientists to pull and clean data from the Global Biodiversity Information Facility (GBIF).
